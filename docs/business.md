# Business Context & Value

## Problem
Enterprise environments require audit logging for compliance (NIST, ISO, PCI). Improper lifecycle management creates operational failures and cost risk.

## Scenario
A versioned S3 bucket accumulated 145K+ objects, preventing infrastructure teardown.

## Business Impact
- Increased storage cost
- Deployment delays
- Operational overhead

## Value Delivered
- Restored deploy/destroy capability
- Reduced operational friction
- Demonstrated lifecycle-aware security design
